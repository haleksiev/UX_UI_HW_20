The Street Dog Coalition Website Redesign Case Study

Research & Discovery

Our process began with thorough UX research to identify key pain points and user needs within the existing “The Street Dog Coalition” website. Through interviews and usability testing, we uncovered navigation challenges and areas lacking visual clarity. These insights informed our redesign strategy, focusing on creating an intuitive, mobile-first experience that prioritized ease of access across all devices.

Mobile-First Approach

We adopted a mobile-first design strategy to ensure “The Street Dog Coalition” website delivered a seamless user experience across all screen sizes. With mobile usage continuing to grow, it was crucial that the redesign not only maintained functionality but also improved aesthetics and usability on smaller devices. This approach provided a strong foundation for a responsive and scalable design.

UI & Visual Enhancements

Leveraging the findings from our UX research, we overhauled the UI with a focus on visual appeal and positive user interactions. By simplifying the navigation and introducing a clean, uplifting design, we created a site that felt welcoming and easy to use. The new design strikes a balance between aesthetics and functionality, ensuring that users can easily find the resources they need while enjoying a visually engaging experience.
